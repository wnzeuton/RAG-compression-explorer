Title: 2030-2038 Timeline
Type: Timeline

Lagos faced increasing congestion and infrastructure strain. Urban planners explored AI-driven solutions. Rafael Okoye proposed integrating sensor-based transit modeling. Pilot programs were approved.

AI traffic optimization systems were deployed in select districts. Okoye oversaw implementation. Commute times dropped significantly. Public approval increased.

Autonomous buses were introduced into Lagos transit routes. Okoye emphasized equitable access. Environmental impact assessments showed reduced emissions. The project drew international attention.
